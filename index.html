Hello! I am a postdoctoral Fellow at Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier, France. I completed my PhD in 2020 in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Patna (IIT Patna) under the supervision of Dr. Sriparna Saha and Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya. My research interest includes automatic text summarization and scholarly data mining using machine learning, evolutionary algorithms, and multi-objective optimization.
